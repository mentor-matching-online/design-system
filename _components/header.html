--- title: Header excerpt: "Headers appear at the top of every page." layout: design-system meta-info: false categories: - components ---

The header shows users that they are on the website and other things they can visit. It is included in the default page template.

When to use this component

Use the header on every page.

Formatted example
Civil Service LGBT+ Network
  • News
  • Events
  • Topics
  • Publications
  • Consultations
  • Networks
  • Our team
  • About
  • Join us
